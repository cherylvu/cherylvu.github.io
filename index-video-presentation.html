About Me

Hi! My name is Cheryl Nguyen.

I was born and raised in Windsor, CT. My parents had immigrated to the United States after the Vietnam War in 1975 and met in the States, where they had my older sister, me, and my younger brother.

I contribute a lot of my independence by my upbringing; I played in the forest in my backyard and grew up exploring, fishing, biking, and travelling. In my early 20's, I've lived in New York City, Philadelphia, and have travelled all over Europe and the States.

I've met many amazing people in my journey who have shaped me into the person I now am today.
