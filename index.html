Are we the right fit?

I am perfect for start-ups or small to mid-size tech consultancies who are not quite ready to hire a full-time bookeeper. The following check points are designed to gauge if my services will compliment your company’s needs. Are the following statements true for your business?

I use...

  • desktop software like Quickbooks, online software like Xero or I have an Excel file.

I have...

  • an accountant, a payroll company, or I am small enough, I don't need either.

I'm here...

  • because I know how important it is to manage my finances but I don't have the time and/or skill to do it myself.

I am...

  • comfortable with trying new technology to manage my finances and am open to a virtual bookkeeper.

My accounts...

  • are already seperated from my business and personal expenses and I only have one A/R.

My business...

  • is currently a small consultancy with no inventory of just myself and/or up to 25 people on staff.
